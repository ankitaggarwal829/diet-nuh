Our Objective

  • To provide quality pre-service teacher education through the D.El.Ed. program, equipping future teachers with professional skills, pedagogical knowledge, and ethical values.
  • To conduct in-service training programs for primary and upper primary school teachers, ensuring continuous professional development and capacity building.
  • To promote child-centered and inclusive education by encouraging innovative teaching methods that cater to diverse learning needs.
  • To act as a resource and support center for schools in the district by providing academic guidance, model lesson plans, and teaching-learning materials.
  • To undertake action research and experimentation in classroom practices, school improvement, and education delivery to strengthen the quality of learning.
  • To facilitate the effective implementation of national and state educational policies, especially the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, at the grassroots level.
